Reading · Okuma

📖 Mini Story: Emre's Holiday

Read the story. If you like, read it out loud — that is great speaking practice too!

Emre is eleven years old. In August, he goes on holiday with his family. They travel to Antalya by bus. The journey is long, but Emre listens to music and looks at the sea.

Every morning, Emre plays football on the beach with new friends. He is a fast striker, and he scores two goals on the first day! In the afternoon, he swims in the sea and eats ice cream.

One evening, the family goes to a restaurant. Emre tries a new food: grilled fish with salad. First he thinks, "I don't like fish." But it is delicious! He eats everything and says, "Thank you, it was great!"

At night, Emre calls his grandmother. "Today I played football, I swam, and I tried new food," he says. "This is the best holiday ever!"

Sounds · Sesler

🗣️ Pronunciation Power

These sounds are tricky for Turkish speakers — but you can do it! Say each word 3 times, slowly then fast. Tap a word to hear it (if your device can speak). 🔊

The "th" sound 👅

Put your tongue softly between your teeth and blow air. It is NOT "t" and NOT "s"!

thanks 🔊 three 🔊 think 🔊 birthday 🔊

The "w" sound 👄

Make your lips a small round circle, like a kiss, then open: "wuh". It is NOT "v"!

water 🔊 we 🔊 window 🔊 sandwich 🔊

Strong final sounds 💥

Don't lose the last sound! Say the end of the word clearly: fooD, playeD, likeT.

food 🔊 played 🔊 liked 🔊 bag 🔊

📖 Story 2: The Big Match

A new story! Read it, then answer the quick-fire questions below.

It is Saturday, and today is the big match! Emre's team, the Blue Lions, plays against the Red Eagles. Emre wakes up early, eats a big breakfast, and puts on his blue shirt with the number 10.

The first half is difficult. The Red Eagles score one goal, and the Blue Lions are sad. At half-time, the coach says, "Don't give up! Play together and run fast!"

In the second half, Emre gets the ball. He runs past two players and passes to his friend Mert. Mert scores! One–one! Then, in the last minute, Emre shoots... GOAL! The Blue Lions win 2–1!

After the match, the team eats pizza together. "Football is better with friends," Emre says with a big smile.
